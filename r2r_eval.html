Talented and gifted:

plot_r2r <-

                                            mean_post_fluency_score = mean(post_fluency_score, na.rm = T)) %>% 
        rename(pre = mean_pre_fluency_score, post = mean_post_fluency_score) %>%

Controlled Difference-In-Difference Estimate

Reshaping the dataset to the proper panel-data format & creating the time (0=pre, 1=post) variable

data_pre <- data %>% select(-post_fluency_score) %>% rename(fluency_score = pre_fluency_score) %>% 
            mutate(time = 0)
data_post <- data %>% select(-pre_fluency_score) %>% rename(fluency_score = post_fluency_score) %>% 
            mutate(time = 1)
data_prepost <- rbind(data_pre, data_post)

Difference-in-difference estimates for:

  1. ready2read all participants,
  2. participants who met half of Ready2Read goals,
  3. participants who met all of Ready2Read goals
